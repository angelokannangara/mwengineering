Fabrication and Welding Ammonia Gas Pipe Line for Vallibel Holdings

Client: Vallibel Holdings (Pvt) Ltd.

Date Completed: 2012

This critical project involved the fabrication, welding, and installation of an ammonia gas pipeline for Vallibel Holdings (Pvt) Ltd.

Due to the nature of ammonia, the project required specialized welding techniques, including Argon Gas welding and 100% X-Ray quality welding, to ensure the highest standards of safety and leak-proof integrity. Our team meticulously executed every phase to deliver a compliant and reliable pipeline system.

The project was successfully completed in 2012, highlighting our expertise in handling hazardous material pipelines with precision and adherence to strict industry standards.

Structure Fabrication, Tank Fabrication and Pipe Fabrication of Dendro Power Plant in Mahiyanganaya for Tokyo Cement Power Lanka Ltd.

Client: Tokyo Cement Power Lanka Ltd.

Date Completed: 2013

This extensive project for Tokyo Cement Power Lanka Ltd. in Mahiyanganaya involved the comprehensive fabrication of structures, various tanks, and pipelines for a Dendro power plant.

The New Dendro Power Plant boasts a 5MW Capacity and represents an investment of 1.5 Billion in Mahiyanganaya. Through its subsidiary Tokyo Cement (Power) Lanka Ltd., this plant aims to sell electricity to the Ceylon Electricity Board.

The construction work for the plant was completed in 2013, and it was successfully commissioned to the national grid in the same year. Our involvement ensured that all fabricated components met the high standards required for power generation infrastructure, delivering an efficient and operational plant on schedule.
